I made a steak

It was good

I followed Joshua Weissman and Adam Ragusea's recipies for a good steak and holy shit it was good. The whole process too about twenty minutes and no skill. I made a steak that unironically made me want to cry. Shit was so good. It was better then any steak I have had at a restaurant.

I bought a two pack of New York strip for $20. I brought them home, got the skillet ready, seasoned them up, put them on the skillet and basted away. I seared on the first side for two minutes, flipped, seared for one minute and put the butter on. Then I basted for five minutes, flipped and basted for 5 more minutes.

That was it. I put them on the plate pouring the butter sauce on top and bam. I served with a baked potato I made in the microwave.

In retrospect I should have put the butter sauce from the stake on the baked potato. I need to do that next time. I ate it with a vodka drink I made with Goof Juice Gamer Supps and Lemonade.

Shit was good. It was a $12 and twenty minute stake dinner with an alcoholic beverage that was the best I have ever had.

10/10 will stake again.
